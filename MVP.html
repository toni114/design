Microsoft Influencer Dashboard (to B)

Ning Aug, 2016-- Jul,2017 Role: UX/UI Designer Other members:MS CIA-China Team

Context

Microsoft gives the Most Valuable Professional award to people who actively share their technical expertise with the different technology communities related directly or indirectly to Microsoft.

There is a list of the candidates for MVP, and differnent business groups in MS need to check the performance of each candidates and vote for them to select the new mvps

Challenges

  • I have to do from 0 since there is no former website to be based on;

  • For some reasons, it is hard to contact with users to do detailed user research before starting design work;

  • I was the only designer in the team and I had to handle all design stuff and collaborate with other developers in the team.

Organize Requirement

The business group provided us a proposal of the website's goals,and firstly, I tried to figured out all the meanings and business logic of this project. Secondly, I tried to got a list of the available data which could expand/restrict the features of the design. Based on this, before strating, I decided to oragnize more potential requirments of this website.The potential functions are important for we can provide them for the business group and make current version more flexible in changing or adding in the future.

The goals which businsess group provied were based on their business logic but not from the product perspective,so I decided to draw a big picture which based on the whole scenario to determine main functions range. The picture contains 3 main parts: stakeholders\relationship\interaction.

Then I tried to organize all these elements into one picture. Based on the picture I draw a layout and dissussed on it with project manager.

Feedback

Throw the stone in water--Although we couldn't do user research before designing, we could send fast design outcome to the business group and collected feedbacks, and from feedbacks we could "extract" their requirements to modify the design and meanwhile gave our advice in return.

Feedback(v1&v2)

  1. The indicator in the website is not suitable

    not straightforward and may coufuse users for it is similar to the indicator of skype(MS chattting tool),and it is not friendly to people with defective color vision.

  2. need vote in homepage
  3. change the color of vote button in profile page
  4. change the rader pic in profile page
  5. One type of score is not enough（addded requirement）
  6. The showing of the vote situation is not suitable
    (number and proportion issue)

Insight of the feedbacks

  • 1、4、6--> more straighfoward in businees
  • 2--> quick vote(important scenario)
  • 3--> highlight the vote function
  • 5---> learn more about business scenario

Iteration-Version2 / Version3

1. To complete quick vote， we need to see there was a change in workflow and based on this change, we got V2 design, after discussion, we got V3 design which also emphasize the function of vote.

  • However, this version has the problem of there were too many colors so that this design may lose its focus.

    • 2.The version 2 design changed the button of vote in profile page and both combine the quick vote function which mentioned before with the vote all funtion in V1.

    3.In profile page,V3 change the radar pic into bar style so that it can make the developer complete this function faster and it also make the view more straightforward although it looked not so fancy as v1 and v2. Besides, all the vote style follow the modification in homepage.

    After

    After several times of interation, the developers strated to build this website and there were more requirements and feedbacks coming from business group.MVP Dashboard is still running in Microsoft and now it is more functional, some new functions are based on the advice we provide,for example-the concept of negotiation .
    I have been following this case in MS until I left.

    Thoughts

  • Understand the business logic--balance between design and business

    • If I want the design to be great then I cannot just focus on design itself and I have to figure out all the business stuff and scenario as more as possible,and sometimes I have to see the website(product) both from a designer's perspective and a business user's perspective.

  • Collaborate with developers--cost and efficiency

    • The more I know about the coding logic, better I did my design.Especially in the situation of agile development in team. In this way, when I design ths stuff, I can also make balance between visual outcome and cost and efficiency of developing.

  • Design for future--modular& flexibly changeable

    • Since the schedule is tight，the design and process of building almost happen in the same period. Additionally, the requirements from business group change a lot.So it is necessary to make my design be more flexibly changeable and I choose make it all functions closer to modules.
