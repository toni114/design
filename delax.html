DELAX(Drive & Relax)

EMOTIONAL RECOGNITION AND REGULATION SYSTEM DESIGN

Ning Aug, 2017-- Jan,2018 Role: Designer Other members:Zhe WANG/Igloo

Background 设计背景

Road rage is aggressive or angry behavior exhibited by a driver of a road vehicle, which includes rude gestures, verbal insults, physical threats or dangerous driving methods targeted toward another driver or a pedestrian in an effort to intimidate or release frustration.

“路怒症”是指驾驶人员在某些情况下产生的攻击性或愤怒性行为，它包括了粗鲁的手势，言语辱骂，暴力威胁甚至是针对其他驾驶人员的危险驾驶行为。随着世界范围内汽车保有量的增加，加之当代人愈加严重的情绪问题，“路怒症”这一问题也逐渐成为当下生活中的一个重要议题。

Problem Statement 问题阐述

  • 路怒问题首先是情绪问题，研究路怒问题首先要从情绪入手，分析情绪的产生以及调节机制;
  • 其次，要从路怒产生具体情境出发，从情境中找出引发不良情绪的诱因以及可能的解决方法；
  • 最后，结合当下的技术和研究方向，筛选出可行的方法并进行初步的的可用性测试。

  • Firstly,the problem of road rage is a emotional problem, so it is necessary to figure out how emotion happpens, especially negative emotion. Additionally, the exsiting methods to regulate negative emotion are needed to be studied.
  • Secondly, we need to focus on the scenario in which road rage happens and try to find the causes in that scenario and meanwhile find optional solutions in it.
  • Last, we should refer to current techonology and find a workable solutionand do preliminary usability test on it.

    • Study on Emotion情绪研究

  • 美国斯坦福大学心理学教授詹姆斯·格罗斯将情绪的产生归纳成某种高度简化的线性序列结构。他称其为情绪的情态模型,对应情绪产生的不同阶段，有相应的调整策略。
  • Modal Model shows different stages of the generation of emotion, and there are corresponding strategies to regualte.

    • Work on Scenario 情境分析

    场景分析（Analyse Scenario）

  • 对情境内的所有相关元素进行分析罗列。
  • List all the related elements in scenario of "road rage".

    • 用户调研（User Research）

  • 对用户进行情境再现与访谈,并将访谈的路怒回忆进行分类整理，以情态模型的不同阶段元素进行分类。
  • Conduct interview on users and make them recall the scenario，then organize their "memory" and sort them by the steps of modal model.

    • 情绪旅程（Journey map of "emotion"）

    找寻机会点（Find Opportunities）

  • 将情绪的发生阶段与相关情境要素结合。并将整理的用户访谈内容结合分析。
  • Combine different stages of emotion with the elements mentioned before.

  • 机会点：
    1. 事先的认知调整
    2. 正向的视觉提示
    3. 听觉和嗅觉的帮助
    4. 情绪的提示
    5. 语言的排解、人际沟通解决

    • Solution 解决方案

  • 以播放音乐和正向提示为特点，并辅以GPS的车内情绪调节交互系统

  • The incar system which aims at regulating emotion features soft music playing and positive reminders
